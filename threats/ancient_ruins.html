Anceint Ruins


"As you draw near the ancient wall, a sense of foreboding overcomes you. You isn't sure why, but you feel this dusty structure may contain great dangers"


Almost as ubuiquitous as colonies of rimworlders are the broken bones of what used to be. Sometimes, great nations rise up and build strongholds that outlast the people they were meant to protect. Everytime a mechanoid exterminates a colony, the builings and perhaps the riches still stand. Most of them are well hidden, or still well defended. Some have traps in them. Some have mechanoids lying in wait. Some have gold, some have silver, others have rare technology or legendary items. If you can field a strong enough scouting party, you might just be able to take the riches for your self.
