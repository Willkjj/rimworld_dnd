Raiders


“Life is pleasant. Death is peaceful. It's the transition that's troublesome.”

-Isaac Asimov


The most ubiquitous threat on the rim is other humans. We all must survive, and some do so by threat of knife. Whether its bands of spacer pirates, fierce neanderthal tribes, or just desperate cannibals looking for food, someone wants what you have. Typically, tribals attack with numbers, and pirate gangs attack with technology. You can expect them to siege your colony, break through your defenses, or just attack you head on. There is no one best way to defend against raiders, because of the multitude of types of raiders and ways they attack, but it's helpful to have as many guns as possible.
