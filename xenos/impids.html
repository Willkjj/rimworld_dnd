Impids


Fast runners that can spew fire, impids are depressive and struggle with farming and melee combat.


Made for desert life, impid cultures live on many rimworlds and dry deathworlds. Impids are extremely fast-moving, easily tolerate high temperatures, and can spew fire from their mouths. Though they are masters on the sand, impids struggle in other environments. They are terrible with farming. They fight well at range, but in close combat their physical weakness becomes apparent.

Outlanders call them dustrunners because of their speed over open terrain and resistance to heat. Some tribes call them devils because of their horns and because they burn their enemies. Impid tribes' combat tactics usually center on rapid-approach incendiary attacks using both handheld fire weapons and their fire spew ability.

Cold Weakness Carriers of this gene are slightly less comfortable in cold temperatures. Mostly roleplay based.
Heat Super-tolerant Carriers of this gene are much more comfortable in warm temperatures. Mostly roleplay based.
