Dirtmoles


Dirtmoles are extremely capable at digging or mining tasks, but suffer from a sensitivity to light, and have poor eyesight at distance.


With gray skin adapted to artificial light, they thrive in cramped and dark spaces where combat and travel happen over short distances. They are extremely capable at digging or mining tasks. In the open, however, they suffer from a sensitivity to light, slow speed and poor eyesight at distance.

After the first tunnel colonies failed due to the stress of confinement, colonization agencies began genetically altering colonists to live without open space or sunlight. This xenotype is known colloquially as the dirtmoles. Today, dirtmoles have expanded out of their original tunnel homes and rule many confined spaces in the anthrosphere. They can be found in deep mines, cramped low-tech spacecraft, and teeming by the billions in the dark underlayers of countless urbworlds.

Gene List

Gene Description Effect
Intense UV Sensitivity Carriers of this gene have biological compounds in their skin that react dangerously to UV radiation. They are intensely sensitive to sunlight. -20% move speed in direct sunlight
Strong Melee Damage Carriers of this gene do more damage in close-quarters combat. Extra-strong fast-twitch muscle fibers make their strikes accurate and powerful. Melee damage is increased by 150%
Nearsighted Carriers of this gene have difficulty seeing at a distance. Their shooting accuracy at long ranges is reduced. Ranged attack ranges are halved
Dark Vision Carriers of this gene see well in low light and are unaffected penalties related to darkness. They have a reflective layer behind the retina that amplifies their ability to see in the dark. Grants darkvision to the carrier.
Fast wound healing Carriers of this gene heal from wounds twice as fast as normal. Rests take half as long to gain the full effect. Or, gain twice the health from resting.
Indoor Dweller Carriers of this gene have no need for the outdoors and will never feel cooped up no matter how long they stay inside. Roleplay based.
Gray Eyes Carriers of this gene have pale white-gray corneas. Cosmetic
Light Gray Skin Carriers of this produce a light-gray pigment in their skin. Cosmetic
Great Mining Carriers of this gene are very skilled at and are passionate about mining and related tasks. Roleplay based.
