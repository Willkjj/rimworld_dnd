Mechanitor


Before you lies the remains of a massive, ancient exostrider war mechanoid. In its guts, it looks like there is a still-functional transponder. If you could extract the transponder, you could decrypt it to gather valuable information.


Mechanitor are those who are able to create and control their very own mechanoids. The technology required to create mechanitor has been lost to this rimworld, but the creation and control of mechanoids is still in force, if highly uncommon. Most mechanitor are reclusive strangers that don't trust anything but themselves and the work of their own hands. However, some colonies integrate mechanitor into their ranks and help build the support structure needed for a colony wide system of mechanoid creation and control. Even rarer are those who wander across the land with their mechanoids, since the infrastructure needed for stronger mechanoids is hard to miniaturize.
