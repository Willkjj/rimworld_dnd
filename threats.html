Dangers, and what to expect from them


The rest of these pages detail some of the dangers on the rim that you can expect to face. Note that this is not a comprehensive list. The universe is so large and varied that not even a tenth of all the things that could kill you are listed here. But please note that some of the most common are listed and accounted for. From mechanoids to manhunters, tornados to toxic fallout, and raiders to ruins, use this guide to inform you of these dangers out on the rim.

While this list may tell you how to deal with some of these dangers, many are simply too dangerous to deal with head on. Acts of the gods must be avoided, and many of the threats you will encounter are their executioners. However, for the stout of heart and strong of limb, use these pages to your best advantage and fight with valor. May Randy have mercy.
