Xenotypes


Xenotypes, also known as xenohumans, refer to the various subspecies of human. They have a different set of genes, whether by adaptation or gene editing.


At some point in humanity's history, gene editing became a common practice among those with the know how and the funding. Governments engineered perfect soliders, mega-corporations engineered perfect colonists, and some naturally appeared over millennia of natural selection. Whatever the case, xenotypes have been meticulously analyzed and catalogued. The ones listed in these pages are the most common to discover on the rim.
